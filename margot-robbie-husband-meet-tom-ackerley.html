    Thomas Ackerley is a former assistant director and film producer from the United Kingdom.

    Tom Ackerley and his wife Margot Robbie co-founded the production business LuckyChap Entertainment.

    Tom Ackerley got his start in the film industry as an extra in the first three Harry Potter films.

    Tom Ackerley returned to the film industry as a production runner for films like Gambit (2012) and Rush after taking a hiatus from it (2013).

    Margot Robbie husband: Meet Tom Ackerley

    Mr. Ackerley, like his wife, works in the film industry, but as an assistant director and producer behind the scenes rather than in front of the camera. Macbeth, Pride, and The Two Faces of January are among his many credits.

    Recent productions with Margot include the ice-skating drama I, Tonya, in which his wife plays disgraced figure-skater Tonya Harding, the thriller Terminal, and the Oscar-winning feature Promising Young Woman.

    He and Margot are also working on Tank Girl and Barbie film adaptations. What if they combined the two? Anarchy in film form.

    Do they have children together?

    Robbie and Ackerley do not have any children of their own at this time.
