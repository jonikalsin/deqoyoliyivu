    Career and Professional Life

    Ha Seok-jin is a professional South Korean actor. The actor is most famous for his roles in TV drama series including Radiant Office in the year 2017, Drinking Solo in the year 2016, Shark in the year 2013, and 4 Legendary Witches in the year 2014. For his role in 4 Legendary Witches, Seok-jin received two nominations for the “Excellence Award”.

    Further, the actor made his entertainment debut in the year 2005. He started as a commercial model for Korean Air. Then in the same year, he appeared in two TV dramas namely Sad Love Story and Princess Lulu. Then in the year 2006, Seok-jin appeared in Dr. Kkang as the character Kim Jin-kyu and in Korea Secret Agency. The same year the actor played the role of Kang Jae-Koo in the film See You After School.

    Likewise, Seok-jin mostly played supporting and minor roles in the early phase of his career. Eventually, the actor succeeded in bagging bigger roles. Seok-jin then began to hog the much-needed limelight after his award-winning performance in the series I am Happy.

    More Details

    Also, he played the role of one of the leading characters in the sitcom Standby, and following this, Seok-jin ended up with better roles. Some award nominations that he has received are under the category “Best Actor” for his series Legendary Witches and Drinking Solo. These have established him as a rather bankable actor. Apart from films and TV series, as an actor has also frequently collaborated with South Korean artists who he features in their music videos.

    Some earlier collaborations include the one with the musician SG Wannabe. Collaborating with the musician, he appeared in his music videos for songs As We Live and Sin and Punishment.

    Other film projects that he was a part of inincludinghethe  2006 movie Who Slept with Her where he played the role of Kim Tae-yo; the 2007’s Unstoppable Marriage in the role of character Wang Ki-back; the 2008’s Summer Whispers as Yoon-soo; and a cameo appearance as Kang Min-ho in the 2016 film Like for Likes.

    Some recent TV dramas that actor Seok-jin has appeared in include Your House Helper in 2018, Crash Landing on You in 2019; When I Was The Prettiest in 2020. For Crash Landing on You, Seok-jin worked alongside famous actor Hyun Bin as his brother, and for When I Was The Prettiest, Seok-jin played the role of the brother of actor Ji Soo and the husband of actress I’m Soo Hyang.

    Overall, since the year 2005, actor Seok-jin has appeared in a total of 27 TV dramas across numerous broadcasting channels. Then in April 2019, Seok-jin signed with a new agency C-JeS Entertainment.
