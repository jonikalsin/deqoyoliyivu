    The prestigious Oscar-winning entertainment personality Genna Davis is grabbing wide media attention from her breakthrough performance in the movies and TV shows like Beetlejuice, The Accidental Tourist, Thelma & Louise, Commander in Chief, etc. 

    The beautiful and talented actress was born as Virginia ElizabethDavis in Wareham, Massachusetts, USA, on January 21, 1956, under the zodiac sign of Aquarius. She is the daughter of late. Lucille Cook Davis (mother) and late. William F. Davis (father). Genna has a brother named Danforth Davis.

    Concerning the education, Genna attended Wareham High School. Later, she enrolled at New England College. Afterward, she received a Bachelor's degree in drama from Boston University.

    Relationship Status

    As of 2020, it seems like the Beetlejuice actress Geena is living a single life involving in any romantic relationship.  After the tragic separation with former love partners, Davis has kept her personal life details private. 

    The woman is busy with her professional works and prefers to spend quality time with her beloved children. Similarly, Geena who founded the non-profit making organization Geena Davis on Gender in Media is also working actively in order to increase the presence of female characters in media.

    Ex-Partner: Reza Jarrahy

    The 64-years-old Geena began dating a neurosurgeon Reza Jarrahy back in 1998. After dating for three-years, Jarrahy and Geena allegedly tied the wedding knot on September 1, 2001.

    From the marital relationship, the husband-wife duo shared their first kid, a daughter, Alizeh Keshvar Davis Jarrahy on April 10, 2002. Similarly, they became parents for the second time on May 6, 2004, who shared a twin son named Kaiis Steven Jarrahy and Kian William Jarrahy.

    Everything was going fine, but citing some irreconcilable differences Jarrahy filed for divorce in May 2018. According to the documents, the couple separated in November 2017. However, Geena claimed that she and Reza were not legally married.  

    Former Husbands

    Before sharing an intimate life with Dr. Reza, Geena was married thrice in her life. She first linked up romantically with the restaurateur Richard Emmolo in December 1977. After four-years, Emmolo and Davis exchanged the wedding vows on March 25, 1981.

    The previously married pair were living a blissful marital life. However, in February 1983 they separated and later divorced on June 27, 1984. 

    Similarly, the 6-feet-0-inch (183 cm) tall Geena met her second husband an actor, Jeff Goldblum, back in 1985 in the set of the film Transylvania 6-5000. After the first encounter, they shared a good bond and got married on November 1, 1987.

    However, Goldblum and Davis also ended up in divorce in October 1990, it was later finalized in 1991. Likewise, from September 18, 1993, to August 26, 1997, Davis was in a marital relationship with the film director Renny Harlin.
