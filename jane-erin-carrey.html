    Jane Erin Carrey is well-known as the only daughter of Jim Carrey, a well-known actor, and comedian. She later became the lead singer for her own band, “The Jane Carrey Band.”

    As a playback singer, she worked alongside her father on his biography film “Jim Carrey: I Needed Color” in 2017. Furthermore, two of her songs were featured in her father’s film “Dumb and Dumber To.” Living in the shadow of a comedic genius may have made it tough for Jane to carve out her own identity, but she always had her father’s complete support in anything she accomplished.
