    Kubota L3350 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V1902-DA-A diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 113.6 ci [1.9 L] engine, which delivers 40 hp [29.8 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

    Kubota L3350 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 1.9L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, Power steering, wet disc brakes, 3770 lbs [1710 kg] weight, 3420 lbs [1551 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 7.9 Qts [7.5 L] engine oil capacity and 8 forward and reverse gears.

    Kubota L3350 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a Kubota BF800 front-end loader and a Kubota L4540 backhoe.
