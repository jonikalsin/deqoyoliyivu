    Pedro Pascal’s Childhood

    Pedro and his parents departed Chile for the United States in the 1970s under political asylum due to their engagement in an anti-dictatorship movement led by Augusto Pinochet. He grew up in San Antonia, Texas, and was born in Orange County, California. He was a competitive swimmer as a child, qualifying for the Texas state championships at the age of 11. However, he was more interested in acting, therefore he dropped out of competitive swimming after enrolling in theatre class. He studied acting at the Orange County School of the Arts and New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts. He began his theatre career when he was 18 years old.
